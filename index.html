Automated YouTube Publishing Tool

A private, self-hosted application used to create and publish videos to the operator's own YouTube channel(s). It is operated personally by the channel owner and is not offered as a public service or product.

What it does

The application produces complete videos (script, visuals, voiceover, music and final assembly) and uploads them to the operator's own YouTube channel via the YouTube Data API. A human reviews and approves each video before it is published.

What it accesses

It connects to the operator's own Google/YouTube account through Google OAuth 2.0, solely to upload and manage the operator's own videos. It does not access, collect, or process data belonging to viewers or any third party. Its use of Google user data adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
