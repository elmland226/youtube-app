Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the automated content tool (the "App") used to create and publish videos to our own YouTube channel(s) handles data. The App is operated privately by the channel owner and is not offered as a public service.

Who we are

The App is a personal, self-hosted tool that writes, assembles and uploads videos to YouTube channel(s) owned and controlled by the operator. It runs on the operator's own machine; there is no public website account system or user sign-up. Contact: wheelieaverageuk@proton.me.

What data the App accesses

  • Google / YouTube account (operator's own). The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 to obtain authorization to upload and manage videos on the operator's own YouTube channel(s) via the YouTube Data API. It accesses only the operator's own account for the sole purpose of publishing the operator's own content.
  • The App does not access, collect, or process personal data belonging to viewers, subscribers, or any third party.
  • The App does not read, sell, or transfer your contacts, messages, files, or any data unrelated to publishing the operator's own videos.

How Google user data is used

The App's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • OAuth access is used only to upload, schedule, and manage the operator's own YouTube videos and their metadata.
  • Google user data is not used for advertising, sold, or shared with third parties.
  • Google user data is not used to train generalized AI/ML models.
  • Access is limited to what is necessary to provide the publishing function.

Storage and security

  • OAuth credentials (refresh/access tokens) are stored locally on the operator's own machine and are not transmitted to any third party other than Google for authentication.
  • No viewer or visitor data is collected or stored by the App.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share any data with third parties. Videos published by the App are, by the operator's choice, made available on YouTube and are subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

Revoking access

The operator can revoke the App's access at any time via the Google Account permissions page: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
